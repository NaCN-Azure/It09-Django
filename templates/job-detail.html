Full Job Description

WE ARE LOOKING FOR A PART TIME RECEPTIONIST FOR OUR SMALL LUXURY HOTEL IN THE HEART OF THE PEAK DISTRICT- WORKING THURSDAY AND FRIDAY FROM 3PM UNTIL 11.30PM- Must be able to commute.

The management and staff of this popular hotel pride themselves on creating an atmosphere like no other hotel - a really personal service with a 'can do' attitude. We are looking for a candidate with previous reception experience.

As one of our Reception Team, you will ensure that the highest standards of hospitality and welcome are demonstrated at all times on front desk. We are looking for a bright personality - someone who can communicate at all levels and has a great telephone manner. You will be responsible for answering all telephone calls both reservations and general enquiries. You will develop a good knowledge of the hotel's facilities and local knowledge providing our guests with an exceptional level of customer service at all times and promoting the reputation of the hotel. During your shift you will be responsible for ensuring the smooth running of the reception area, managing your time efficiently whilst taking and entering reservations into the Hotel Executive system and dealing with confirmation letters and any other correspondence. Also our restaurant billing is performed by our Receptionists, both cash and credit cards.

The position is 17hrs per week and 2 late shifts every Thursday and Friday. The shift starts at 3pm and finishes at 11.30pm, maybe later on busier nights. We are looking for a very special person who is friendly, professional, polite and bubbly with excellent customer service skills. The ideal candidate must have previous reception experience (not necessarily within a hospitality setting) and a knowledge of Front Office Systems would be a benefit, but not essential. Your application will not be considered if you do not have any form of reception experience.
